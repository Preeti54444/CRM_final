✅ getLenders() Full CRUD access to all lenders
✅ getActiveLenders() Can view active lenders
✅ createLender() Can create new lenders
✅ updateLender() Can update lender details
✅ deleteLender() Can delete lenders
✅ getLenderById() Can view specific lenders

📋 Summary

  • Admin: Full CRUD access to lender management - can create, read, update, and delete all lender records
  • Branch Manager, Sales Executive, Loan Processing Executive: Read-only access to active lenders for reference during lead processing and sales activities
  • Sub-DSA / Connector: No direct lender access - they work through the simplified portal and track their own leads
  • Finance / Accounts: No direct lender access - they focus on payout reports and financial reconciliation

Note: These permissions are enforced at the Firebase data layer and apply to the lender master panel functionality.
